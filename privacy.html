Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how VIE Institute of Education ("School") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from students, parents/guardians, and staff members, including but not limited to:

  • Names and contact details (such as email addresses, phone numbers, and addresses)
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Student records and academic information
  • Health and medical information
  • Photographs or videos taken during school activities or events

How We Use the Information

We may use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing educational services and support
  • Communicating with students, parents/guardians, and staff
  • Assessing student progress and academic performance
  • Ensuring student safety and well-being
  • Organizing school activities and events
  • Complying with legal obligations

Information Security

The School implements appropriate security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

Disclosure to Third Parties

We may disclose personal information to third parties in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or in response to a legal process
  • To our trusted service providers who assist us in delivering our services
  • In case of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets

Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website with the updated Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@vieschool.com
